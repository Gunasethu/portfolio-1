About me

I am an engineer turned self-taught product designer - based out of Tamilnadu. I am a detail-oriented designer who always strives for simplest design possible.

I believe Material design is truly revolutionary and its principles are my core philosophy. Google Maps and Pinterest are my favorite products because former is the best example of how a product can solve for multiple use cases and latter is the example of how a simple & intuitive design can be timeless.
